Bridging Architecture & Planning

I am a dual-qualified Architect and Urban & Regional Planner from the Faculty of Architecture and Ekistics, Jamia Millia Islamia. My work spans from the intimate scale of heritage documentation and building design to the regional scale of transit corridor modelling and land-use simulation.

My academic journey has taken me through Buddhist heritage architecture in Kushinagar, EWS housing policy in Delhi, mixed-use city-centre design in Gorakhpur, urban poverty research in Govindpuri, campus disaster resilience planning, and GIS-based regional growth modelling along the Delhi-Meerut RRTS corridor.
